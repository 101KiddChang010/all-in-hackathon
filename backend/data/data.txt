 ZedongMao Zedong is one of the most famous people in Asia. He was the founder of the People's Republic of China and served as its first Chairman. Mao was born in Hunan province in China in 1893. He was a Marxist-Leninist and a member of the Chinese Communist Party. Mao led China through a period of important economic and social changes, including the collectivization of agriculture and the Great Leap Forward. He also oversaw the Chinese Communist Revolution, which resulted in the creation of the People's Republic of China. Mao died in 1976.
